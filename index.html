About Us

ASB Valiant, a Limited Liability company, began operations in 2007 out of a fervent desire and decisive action to power Africa's growth by building sustainable communities and businesses.

We are a leading Integrated Engineering and Energy Solutions Provider offering our customers in the Oil & Gas, Construction, Marine, Power and Energy sectors innovative products and smart services designed to overcome challenges and deliver long-lasting value.

For several years, we have demonstrated dauntless commitment to solve Africa's changing energy needs by serving our customers and enabling them to build a better future for their consumers and local communities.

What We Do

We are a leading Integrated Engineering and Energy Solutions Provider with the determination, expertise and experience required to design and manage large-scale, turn-key projects.

Power Generation Services

Comprehensive power generation solutions from individual generating sets to complete, ready-to-run power plants.

  • Individual generating sets to complete power plants
  • Full EPC (Engineering, Procurement, Construction)
  • Operations and Maintenance services
  • Work with Major Utilities, Municipal plants, and IPPs

Natural Gas Solutions

Integrated solutions for natural gas conditioning and gas fuel treatment systems.

  • Complete Natural Gas Process Plant
  • Gas Treatment Plant
  • Medium/High Pressure Gas Units
  • Compression System to Maximize Plant Efficiency
  • Pipeline Booster Compression System

EPC Services

Holistic Energy solutions managing the entire project cycle from design to commissioning.

  • Design, Engineering and Procurement

  • Cycle and cogeneration projects
  • Phased delivery options

Value Added Services
